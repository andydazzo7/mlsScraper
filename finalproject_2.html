What to do at the Grand Canyon:

  1. Go hiking
  2. Ride a mule
  3. Ride a train
  4. Take a scenic drive
  5. Go camping in a tent or at an RV park
  6. Go on a rafting trip
  7. Take a helicopter tour
  8. Ride a bike around the rim of the canyon
  9. Stand on the Grand Canyon Skywalk
  10. Take a Desert Jeep Tour
